GRBLduino

I'm working on the design of an all-in-one fabable CNC machine controller based on Fabable GRBL arduino shield, the Fabinabox SPINDLE Controller and the Arduino Duemilanove design.
The goal is to have an arduino based cnc controller as a platform for controlling any GRBL firmware based machine we create in the FabLab. I know there are more innovative ways of controlling CNC machines like the FABnet setup used in the Fabinabox but GRBL seems to be the fastest way to do CNC controlling at the FabLab right know.
